Gamma Physicore and Pain Relief Clinic

Gamma Physio Care is a 20+ beded multi-specility modern health care facility , which attempts to make the experience of a hospital stress free under the circumstances of illness or injuries. It was founded by Dr. Jitender Singla in 2005 and has since grown in stature and reputation.

The hospital operates under a high quality standard. It is only ISO certified clinic in Haryana , spacious and well lit. The corridors and patient cabins have also plenty of sunlight. The diagnosis and treatment of the patients is carried out by highly qualified Senior Physiotherapist's assisted by team of Nursing staff & assistant.

Gamma Physio Care, today serves hundreds of patients with delivering 19+ years of healthcare excellence. Patients from india and local expatriates including our many patrons have been seeking treatment here at Gamma Physio Care. Hence building a strong and trusted repute over the years in the region, Gamma Physio Care and its team of dedicated professionals are proud to be.

Physiotherapy is now an integral part of orthopedic practice. Orthopedic Neuro, cardio, padeiatric etc practics is one of the major specialties of the science of physiotherapy and plays a significant role in restoring full function at the best. Physiotherapy is a practical profession. It is composed of manual, mechanical & electrotherapy techniques. The effects of these are regulated by the individualized dosimetry prescribed & implemented by the physiotherapist. There are so many hospitals in the city but no where the PT department according to the patients requirement, Thus ,we felt the need for the one & tried to bring out an advanced PT clinic.
