Welcome To

Gamma Physicore and Pain Relief Clinic

Gamma Physio Care is a state-of-the-art, multi-specialty healthcare facility with over 30 beds, committed to providing a stress-free hospital experience for patients facing illness or injury. Established in 2005 by Dr. Jitender Singla, the hospital has consistently expanded its reputation for excellence in patient care.

Operating under the highest quality standards, Gamma Physio Care is the only ISO-certified clinic in Haryana. The facility is designed to ensure a comfortable environment with spacious, well-lit corridors and patient rooms, all benefiting from ample natural sunlight. Patient diagnosis and treatment are provided by a team of highly qualified senior physiotherapists, supported by a dedicated staff of nurses and assistants.

Gamma Physio Care is also the sole authorized clinic by Govt. Of India for the treatment of ECHS (Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme) beneficiaries, including ex-servicemen and their dependents.

With nearly two decades of healthcare excellence, Gamma Physio Care continues to serve more than twenty thousands of patients, including those from Palwal and adjoining cities. The hospital has earned a trusted reputation in the region, with many loyal patrons returning for care. The Gamma Physio Care team takes great pride in delivering exceptional healthcare services to the community.

Dr. Jitender Singla

B.P.T., M.P.T. (Ortho)

With over 19 years of experience, Dr. Jitender Singla is a highly skilled physiotherapist specializing in orthopedic care. He holds a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Physiotherapy and founded Gamma Physiocare to provide patients with personalized, high-quality treatment. Dr. Singla’s extensive expertise spans musculoskeletal conditions, post-surgical rehabilitation, and chronic pain management, helping many individuals achieve pain-free living and improved mobility.

Having attended numerous national and international physiotherapy conferences, Dr. Singla remains at the forefront of the latest advancements in the field. His holistic approach, combined with evidence-based techniques, allows him to create tailored care plans for each patient. Dr. Singla’s commitment to improving his patients’ quality of life makes him a trusted name in physiotherapy.

We also provide Physiotherapy facility at home and other prosthetic & orthotic requirement of patients.
